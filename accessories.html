These are items I like to have with me when I have a cold:

Masks

We’re still in a pandemic, so having masks is vital, especailly when you’re sick. Personally, I think it is common courtesy to wear a mask when you're sick, that way it lowers your chances of spreading germs to people around you.

Mask image: wear the mask by Victoruler at https://thenounproject.com/term/wear-the-mask/3339087/, CC BY 3.0.

Scarf and Gloves

I know this is a given, especially during the cold weather, but there have been plenty of times where I’ve thought to myself, “I won’t need this, it’s not that cold,” and then regret it immediately after. Stay comfy and warm!
